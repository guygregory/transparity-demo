Holiday Mini-Game

Catch the Snowflake

Glide a mitten across the snow and collect the falling flakes before they melt.

How to play

  • Use mouse/touch to move the mitten.
  • Or use keyboard arrows / A-D.
  • Catch snowflakes; avoid letting 5 fall.
  • Collect as many as you can in 60s.

Accessibility

Keyboard friendly, no rapid flashing. Score updates are announced for screen readers.

Built for a cozy holiday moment. Runs entirely in your browser.
